AAR Marks Side Frame 470A

6.3 Marking
6.3.1 Effective July 1, 2013, bar code labels must be affixed to all new bolsters and side frames in accordance with MSRP, Section F, Standard S-920.
6.3.2 Side frames and bolsters shall have legible markings as indicated in MSRP, Section S, Part II, Standards S-312 and S-314. AAR identification number must be cast integral to the castings.
6.3.2.1 When any of the required markings shown in MSRP Standards S-312 and S-314 are illegible, the manufacturer must apply these markings to each casting in a workmanlike manner by electric arc welding using a maximum 1/8 in. diameter electrode. Subsequent heat-treatment is not required for pencil welding of markings in Zones 2 and 3, as defined in Figs. 8.1 and 8.2.
6.3.2.2 When the AAR identification number is illegible, the manufacturer must apply this marking using pencil welding as specified in paragraph 6.3.2.1. To ensure that the intent of paragraph 6.3.2 and MSRP Standards S-312 and S-314 is satisfied, casting production, as indicated by the cast date, must be subsequent to the date on which the AAR identification number was granted.
