Negative surface conditions:
a) Minimum metal thickness is maintained for all conditions.
b) Zone 2 and zone 3 areas:
i) 1/2” or less in diameter and 3/16” or less in depth—are acceptable.
ii) Greater than 1/2” in diameter and 3/16” or less in depth are blended.
iii) Greater than 3/16” in depth are weld repaired.
c) Zone 1 areas:
i) Caulking method of repair (mechanical deformation of metal) is prohibited.
ii) 1/2” or less in diameter and 1/8” or less in depth are acceptable provided the edges are not sharp or jagged.
iii) Greater than 1/2” in diameter and 1/8” or less in depth are blended.
iv) Greater than 1/8” in depth are weld repaired.
d) Zone 0 areas:
i) Caulking method of repair (mechanical deformation of metal) is prohibited.
